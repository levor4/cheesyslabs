Cheesy’s Labs

The twisted tale behind the mutation outbreak

In Cheesy’s Labs, a lab explosion merges the main character with the mysterious substance Vlux, turning him into a dangerous Vluxling. The mutation spreads rapidly, changing humans and their surroundings into grotesque new forms.

The player is trapped inside the facility, discovering tapes that reveal the tragic events behind the outbreak.
